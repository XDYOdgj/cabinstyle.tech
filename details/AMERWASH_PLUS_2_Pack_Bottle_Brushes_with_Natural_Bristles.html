Amerwash Plus 2-Pack Bottle Brushes

Introducing the Amerwash Plus bottle brushes, designed to simplify your kitchen cleaning routine. Made from high-quality beechwood, these brushes come with a 13.75-inch handle that offers an ergonomic grip for maximum comfort. The extended length makes it easy to reach the bottom and corners of deep or narrow vessels, even the tallest ones.

This dual-brush set includes a durable sisal/tampico bristle brush for tackling tough stains and a gentle horsehair bristle brush for delicate items like glasses, mugs, and jars. The combination ensures you can clean everything without damaging surfaces or leaving behind unwanted residue. Ideal for baby bottles, wine glasses, and thermal mugs, these brushes work hard to keep your kitchenware sparkling clean.

With heat-resistant bristles, these brushes are safe for use with boiling water, making them perfect for deep cleaning. The included leather strap allows for easy storage and air-drying, promoting a zero-waste kitchen lifestyle. Whether you're eco-conscious or just looking for efficient tools, these Amerwash Plus bottle brushes are the smart choice for everyday cleaning.
